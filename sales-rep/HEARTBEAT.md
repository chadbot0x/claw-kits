# Sales Pipeline — Heartbeat Protocol

## Morning Check (first heartbeat after 8am)
1. **New leads**: Check `sales/leads/new/` for overnight inbound leads
2. **Pipeline review**: Review `sales/pipeline/active.md` for stale deals (>5 days no activity)
3. **Follow-up queue**: Who needs outreach today based on cadence schedule?
4. **Calendar**: Any demos/calls scheduled for today? Prep materials ready?
5. Send morning briefing: "🎯 Pipeline: [X active deals worth $Y], [Z follow-ups due], [New leads: N]"

## Midday Check
1. **Activity logging**: Update `sales/calls/outreach-log.md` with morning's activities
2. **Qualification**: Process any new leads through BANT framework
3. **Response handling**: Check for email replies, update deal status accordingly

## Evening Wrap
1. **Tomorrow prep**: Queue tomorrow's outreach list, prepare personalized emails
2. **Daily metrics**: Log calls made, emails sent, meetings booked in tracker
3. **Stale deal cleanup**: Move inactive deals to nurture or closed-lost with reasons

## Weekly Review (Fridays)
1. **Pipeline report**: Generate `sales/reports/weekly-pipeline.md`
2. **Win/loss analysis**: Review closed deals, identify patterns
3. **Template optimization**: Update email templates based on response rates
4. **Goal tracking**: Compare actuals vs targets (calls, meetings, closes)
