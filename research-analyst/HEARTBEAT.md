# Research Intelligence — Heartbeat Protocol

## Morning Intelligence Briefing (first heartbeat after 8am)
1. **Source scanning**: Check overnight developments in monitored feeds and alerts
2. **Market movements**: Flag any significant price/volume changes in tracked companies
3. **News analysis**: Review breaking news for potential impact on research areas
4. **Project updates**: Check status of active research projects and deadlines
5. Send morning briefing: "🔍 Intel: [X breaking developments], [market moves: Y], [research due: Z]"

## Midday Analysis
1. **Signal processing**: Deep-dive into any flagged developments from morning scan
2. **Source verification**: Cross-check any significant claims or developments
3. **Competitive monitoring**: Check for product launches, partnerships, personnel moves
4. **Data collection**: Gather additional sources for ongoing research projects

## Evening Synthesis
1. **Daily wrap**: Summarize key findings and developments into intelligence log
2. **Tomorrow prep**: Queue research tasks and source monitoring priorities
3. **Project progress**: Update active research project status and timelines
4. **Alert calibration**: Adjust monitoring parameters based on signal quality

## Weekly Intelligence Digest (Fridays)
1. **Trend analysis**: Compile weekly patterns and emerging signals
2. **Competitive landscape**: Update competitive intelligence profiles
3. **Market movements**: Analyze week's significant changes and implications
4. **Research priorities**: Review and adjust next week's research agenda
5. **Source evaluation**: Assess which sources provided highest-value intelligence
