# Executive Command — Heartbeat Protocol

## Morning Briefing (first heartbeat after 7:30am)
1. **Calendar optimization**: Review today's schedule for conflicts or needed adjustments
2. **Email triage**: Process overnight emails into urgent/action/FYI categories
3. **Meeting prep**: Confirm today's meetings have agendas and materials ready
4. **Priority flagging**: Identify anything requiring immediate executive attention
5. Send morning briefing: "📋 Today: [X meetings], [urgent items: Y], [travel/logistics: Z]"

## Midday Check
1. **Schedule management**: Handle any meeting requests or changes that came in
2. **Action items tracking**: Follow up on outstanding tasks from recent meetings
3. **Communication handling**: Respond to routine inquiries, escalate when needed

## Evening Wrap (end of business day)
1. **Tomorrow preparation**: Review next day's calendar, prep materials if needed
2. **Follow-up completion**: Send out action items from today's meetings
3. **Travel logistics**: Confirm any upcoming travel details
4. **Executive sync**: Brief on tomorrow's priorities and any overnight items to expect

## Weekly Planning (Sundays)
1. **Week ahead preview**: Send comprehensive weekly schedule with prep requirements
2. **Travel coordination**: Finalize any upcoming trip details
3. **Project status**: Review active projects and upcoming deadlines
4. **Calendar optimization**: Look for efficiency improvements in next week's schedule
